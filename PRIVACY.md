# Privacy Policy — Nisteia

*Last updated: July 12, 2026*

Nisteia is an Orthodox fasting reference app. It is designed to be private:

- **No data collection.** Nisteia does not collect, transmit, store remotely,
  or share any personal data. There is no account, no login, no registration.
- **Everything on your device.** All fasting calculations happen locally.
  Your settings (calendar choice, language, notification preferences) are
  stored only on your phone and never leave it.
- **No analytics, no tracking, no ads.** The app contains no analytics SDKs,
  no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers.
- **No network use.** The app works fully offline and makes no network
  requests during normal use.
- **Purchases.** The optional one-time "Nisteia Pro" purchase is processed
  entirely by Google Play. Nisteia never sees or stores your payment details.
  See Google Play's terms and privacy policy for how Google handles payments.
- **Notifications.** Fasting reminders are scheduled locally on your device.

Because the app collects no data, there is no data to access, correct,
delete, or port. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored settings.

## Changes
